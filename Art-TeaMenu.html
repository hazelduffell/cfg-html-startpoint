Art-Tea Café Menu

Every artist requires some food for thought…plus a bit of caffeine! Try our Art-Tea twists on your favourite drinks and cakes!

    Picassoppuccino:

    A hazelnut-flavoured cappuccino layered with a dash of spice and served with freshly whipped cream and a flake. Give your taste buds a treat that would have made Picasso himself blush with excitement with our beautiful Picassoppuccino!

    Ertespresso:

    An espresso with a dash of strawberry syrup and served with freshly whipped cream and a flake. A colourful twist on the traditional Espresso. Espress yourself with an Ertespresso; let your creativity run wild!

    Dali White:

    A rich, full flavoured coffee with added coconut syrup and drizzled with shortbread. Served with a wafer and adorned with marshmallows. A frazzling twist on the traditional Flat White. Enjoy this unconventional taste; only few will recognise the true beauty of the creative mix of flavours!

    Monet Latte:

    Smooth, creamy drink with a shot of espresso and covered with honey syrup. Served with freshly whipped cream, dusted with chocolate and comes with a free gingerbread biscuit. Roll in the Monet with our palette twist on the traditional Latte. Check out Art-Tea’s exciting rendition of the nation’s beloved beverage. Not tried it already? It’s never too Latte to Monet!

    Renoir-Tea:

    Enjoy the rich taste of organic Oolong green tea dusted with chocolate and served with a slice of lemon. Coffee not your thing? Refresh your mind with our Art-Tea Renoir Green Tea.

To see our full list of beverages and cakes, please visit our store!
